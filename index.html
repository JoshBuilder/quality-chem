Built on Experience. Driven by Quality.

Quality Chem & Consulting Corp LLC has been a trusted name in the chemical manufacturing industry for more than two decades. Founded and led by Chris McCallister, our company was built on a simple principle: deliver high-quality products and treat every customer like a partner.

From our facility in Melbourne, FL, we produce a diverse range of proprietary cleaners, industrial lubricants, degreasers, and specialty chemicals that serve customers across multiple industries. Whether you need a proven off-the-shelf solution or a custom-blended formula tailored to your exact specifications, our team has the expertise and equipment to get it done right.

What sets us apart is the personal touch. As a small, owner-operated business, we're able to offer the kind of responsive, one-on-one service that larger chemical companies simply can't match. When you call Quality Chem, you're talking to people who know your account, understand your needs, and care about getting your order out the door on time.
